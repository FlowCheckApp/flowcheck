What Is FlowCheck

The Standard
FlowCheck should feel like a private money companion, not a finance app fighting for attention.

That principle should show up everywhere: in the brand, in the product design, in the copy, and in the way people move from website to app without friction.

Security

Trust has to feel clear, not hidden in fine print.

FlowCheck uses the same Firebase account system across web and mobile, and Plaid handles bank credential entry. The goal is a secure flow that still feels understandable to a normal person.

One account system

People can create an account on the web and use the same credentials in the app without confusion.

Read-only bank sync

Plaid handles bank credentials directly. FlowCheck does not store a user’s bank password or move their money.

Clear support paths

The site now includes cleaner About, Story, and Contact pages so the brand feels more established and easier to trust.
