Service

FlowCheck is a personal finance app designed to help you understand spending, recurring charges, and cash-flow planning. It is not a bank, lender, broker, or investment advisor, and nothing in the app is financial, tax, or legal advice.

Subscriptions

FlowCheck Premium is offered as an auto-renewable subscription through Apple. The purchase flow shows the subscription title, billing length, trial terms, and price before purchase. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

Bank Connections

Optional bank connections are powered by Plaid. FlowCheck receives read-only transaction, balance, and account metadata. FlowCheck cannot initiate transfers or move money on your behalf.

Recurring Charge Detection

Subscription, bill, transfer, and debt classifications are generated from transaction patterns. They are estimates for convenience and should be reviewed before you rely on them for cancellation, budgeting, or repayment decisions.
