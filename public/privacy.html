Privacy Policy

How FlowCheck handles your data, connected accounts, and subscription-related information.
We do not sell your personal data.

Data We Collect

FlowCheck stores what you provide, such as your name, email address, income, goals, bills, and optional connected-account data through Plaid. The app may also infer recurring charges from your transaction history to organize your money flow.

How Data Is Stored

Most budgeting data is stored locally on your iPhone. Authentication is handled by Firebase. Optional connected-account metadata and Plaid access tokens are stored server-side so FlowCheck can sync securely. We never store your banking password.

Bank Account Connections

If you link a financial account, Plaid securely handles credential entry. FlowCheck receives read-only transaction, balance, and account metadata. FlowCheck cannot move money or initiate transfers.

Biometrics

Face ID is handled by Apple Secure Enclave on your device. FlowCheck does not collect or store your biometric data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data for advertising. Data is only shared with services required to run features you use, such as Apple subscriptions, Plaid account linking, Firebase authentication, RevenueCat subscription state, or opted-in emails.

Deletion

You can delete your FlowCheck account and synced data inside the app from Settings. Deleting your account removes Firebase sign-in access and connected-account records tied to your profile.
