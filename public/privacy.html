FlowCheck stores your financial data locally on your device. We do not sell your data, run ads, or share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide core features (Plaid for bank connections, RevenueCat for purchases). You are always in control.

3
How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve FlowCheck's features:

  • Display your financial dashboard — show spending summaries, budgets, net worth, and transactions
  • Calculate budgets and savings goals — using the income and expense data you provide
  • Provide bank sync — via Plaid to automatically import transactions when you connect an account
  • Manage your subscription — via RevenueCat to verify premium access
  • Improve the app — using anonymized, aggregated crash and usage data from Apple's standard iOS analytics

✅ We never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose other than providing FlowCheck's features directly to you.

4
Data Storage & Security

Local storage by default. All financial data you enter into FlowCheck is stored locally on your device using iOS device storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use a feature that requires it (such as Plaid bank sync).

iCloud Backup (optional). If you enable iCloud Backup within FlowCheck settings, your app data may be included in your iCloud backup, governed by Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy.

Security measures:

  • iOS device encryption (AES-256) protects all locally stored data at rest
  • Face ID / Touch ID biometric authentication for app access (when enabled)
  • All network communications use HTTPS / TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Plaid's bank-grade security for all financial institution connections
  • We never store your banking credentials — Plaid handles authentication directly with your bank

6
Bank Account Connections (Plaid)

When you connect a bank account, FlowCheck uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. Here is exactly how it works:

  • You enter your bank credentials directly into Plaid's secure interface — FlowCheck never sees your username or password
  • Plaid returns read-only transaction and balance data to FlowCheck
  • FlowCheck stores that data locally on your device only
  • FlowCheck cannot initiate payments, transfers, or any write actions to your bank account
  • You can disconnect any linked account at any time from Settings → Linked Accounts

By connecting a bank account you also agree to Plaid's End User Privacy Policy. Bank connections are optional — you may use FlowCheck entirely with manually entered transactions.

7
In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

FlowCheck offers a Premium subscription through Apple's in-app purchase system, managed by RevenueCat, Inc.

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple — we never receive or store your payment card information
  • RevenueCat receives a non-personally-identifiable app user ID and your purchase status to verify premium access
  • To manage or cancel your subscription, go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

9
Data Retention & Deletion

Your data, your control. Because FlowCheck stores data locally on your device, you are always in full control.

To delete all your FlowCheck data:

  • Open FlowCheck → More tab → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete All Data
  • Or simply delete the FlowCheck app — all locally stored data is immediately and permanently removed

Third-party data: To remove your data from Plaid or RevenueCat, contact them directly:

  • Plaid: plaid.com/legal or privacy@plaid.com
  • RevenueCat: revenuecat.com/privacy

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — request that we delete your personal information (see Section 9)
  • Right to correction — request that we correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to opt out of sale — we do not sell personal data, so this right is automatically fulfilled
  • Right to data portability — export your transaction data as CSV from the Transactions page
  • Right to non-discrimination — exercising privacy rights will not affect your access to FlowCheck

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.

European users (GDPR): Our lawful basis for processing is your consent and our legitimate interest in providing the service you requested. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting the app.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at flowcheckapp.help@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days.
