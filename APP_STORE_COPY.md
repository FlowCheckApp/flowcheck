## Description
FlowCheck helps you understand what is safe to spend before your next paycheck.

Built for calm money clarity, FlowCheck turns your income, bills, recurring charges, and live account activity into one simple daily plan.

With FlowCheck, you can:
- See what is safe to spend today
- Link your bank securely with read-only sync
- Catch recurring charges automatically
- Understand bills and card payments in one place
- Get a calmer view of your money without budget overload

FlowCheck is designed for people who want a more elegant, less stressful way to manage money.

Privacy first:
- Read-only bank sync through Plaid
- Your bank password stays with your bank
- Secure sign-in with modern account protection

Subscription details:
FlowCheck Premium is an auto-renewable subscription. Pricing may vary by plan and region. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel anytime in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
